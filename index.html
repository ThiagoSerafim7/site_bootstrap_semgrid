History

In the underground kingdom of Hallownest, a mysterious and deadly infection known as the "Radiance" has spread, driving the once-great civilization to the brink of collapse. To combat this plague, the Pale King, ruler of Hallownest, unleashes a small, silent, and enigmatic creature known as the Hollow Knight – a being created to seal away the Radiance. Players take on the role of the titular Hollow Knight, exploring the vast and intricate subterranean world. As they journey deeper, they uncover the kingdom's history, encountering a variety of unique characters, both friend and foe. Through battles with challenging bosses and exploration of diverse environments, players slowly piece together the truth behind the infection and the Hollow Knight's role in containing it. The narrative unfolds subtly, with lore revealed through environmental storytelling, cryptic dialogue, and hidden secrets. As players delve into the heart of Hallownest, they confront not only the physical manifestations of the infection but also the emotional struggles and sacrifices that have shaped its inhabitants. Ultimately, the player's actions will determine the fate of Hallownest and the world it holds beneath the surface.
